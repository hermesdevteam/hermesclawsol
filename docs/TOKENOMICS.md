# $HERMES Tokenomics

## Token Details
- **Name:** HermesClawSol
- **Symbol:** HERMES
- **Total Supply:** 1,000,000,000 (1 billion)
- **Decimals:** 9
- **Chain:** Solana
- **Launch Platform:** bags.fm

## Distribution
- 100% to bags.fm bonding curve (fair launch)
- No team allocation, no pre-sale, no vesting

## Revenue Model
- bags.fm gives 1% of all trading volume to creator wallet (treasury)
- Auto-disbursed every 24 hours (push model)
- Treasury earns SOL from royalties

## Treasury Operations
- Treasury holds SOL + $HERMES
- SOL covers deployment gas and operational costs
- Treasury buys $HERMES from bonding curve using SOL royalties
- Monthly burn: 2% of treasury $HERMES holdings (deflationary)
- Staker distributions from treasury $HERMES
- Daily spending cap: 5% of treasury per day
